The Impossible List

by Issam Echahbouni

You might be wondering what an impossible list is. Unlike a bucket list, it is an ever-evolving document of goals that grows as you grow. As soon as you cross something off, you add new milestones. It is meant to be a roadmap of continuous personal and technical evolution.

I keep this list public to maintain accountability, push my boundaries in cybersecurity, and inspire others to construct their own.

Cybersecurity & Certifications

  • Obtain Google/Cisco Cybersecurity Foundations April 2026
  • Obtain eJPT (Junior Penetration Tester) certification
  • Obtain OSCP (Offensive Security Certified Professional)
  • Deploy a complete automated malware analysis sandbox lab
  • Reach top 1% in Hack The Box / TryHackMe
  • Publish and register a CVE (Vulnerability disclosure)

Technology & Home Labs

  • Deploy custom SNORT IPS rule-sets in NFQ mode on a personal router
  • Build a multi-agent Zabbix monitoring server for local system supervision
  • Develop a custom Python-based active network auditing tool

Academic & Career

  • Graduate from EST Tétouan with top honors
  • Secure a late 2026 practical cybersecurity initiation internship
  • Obtain an engineering degree in network security and auditing

Personal & Traveling

  • Travel to Spain & establish regional security contacts
  • Build a home server rack for continuous virtualization simulations
  • Develop a technical blog to document writeups and lab setups
